League of Legends

What is League of Legends?

League of Legends is a role-playing MMORPG where you play with nine other people. You play on a team with five versus another team of five. There is a wide variety of champions to play:

    Ranged/Melee
    Attack Damage/Magic Damage
    Tank/Bruiser
    Assassins
    Enchanter

The objective of the game is to farm minions and get stronger in order to overcome and destroy the enemy's nexus.

Roles

There are different roles in League of Legends such as:

    Top
    Jungle
    Mid
    Bottom (which can also be called ADC or APC)
    Support

Platforms to play League of Legends

You can play League of Legends on mobile and PC.
